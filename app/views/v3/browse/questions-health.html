Questions about your health

If you need to speak to someone:

  • speak to a pharmacist for advice on medicines or common problems like coughs, colds and rashes
  • see your GP or dentist

To find out more about conditions and medicines:

  • use the NHS website health A-Z to get more information on conditions, symptoms and treatments, including what to do and when to get help
  • use the NHS website medicines A-Z to find out how your medicine works, how and when to take it, possible side effects and answers to your common questions
