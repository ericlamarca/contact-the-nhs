Make a complaint about an NHS service

Contact NHS England if your complaint is about:

  • a GP surgery
  • an NHS dentist
  • an NHS optician
  • a pharmacy
  • NHS 111

Contact your local CCG if your complaint is about:

  • hospital care
  • mental health services
  • out of hours services
  • community services such as district nursing
Information:

You can speak to the Patient Advice and Liaison service (PALS) for advice if your complaint is about care you received in hospital.
