Eligibility and costs

Am I eligible for free NHS treatment?

Although most NHS treatment is free, there's usually a charge for:

  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS dental check-ups and treatment
  • sight tests
  • new glasses or contact lenses
  • repairing or replacing glasses or contact lenses
  • NHS wigs

  • travel for NHS treatment
