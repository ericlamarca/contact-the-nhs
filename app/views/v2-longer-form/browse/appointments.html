Appointments

If you need to see a GP

You can call the phone number for your local GP surgery to book, change or cancel an appointment.

Many GP surgery's now have GP online services, which also allow you to book, change or cancel your appointment.

Find your nearest GP surgery

If it's a hospital appointment

If you've been referred by a GP for an appointment at hospital you should get a letter in the post. This letter will tell you when the appointment will be.

On the letter, there should be a phone number that you can call if you need to change or cancel your appointment.

If you cannot find your letter or do not have one search here to find contact details for the hospital.

e-Referral Service

If you have been referred to a hospital or clinic through the NHS e-Referral Service you may be able to use this to book, change or cancel your appointment.

You'll get a letter in the post with instructions on how to how to login and use the NHS e-Referral Service.

Find out more about using the e-Referral Service.
