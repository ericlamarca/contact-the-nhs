Your medical record and NHS number

Get access to your medical records

You can use online GP services to access information about your medication, allergies, vaccinations, previous illnesses and test results, hospital discharge summaries, appointment letters and referral letters.

If you have access to online service already you'll need to complete a registration form at the GP's office (include link to more info).

Where to find your NHS number

You can find your NHS number on any letters you’ve had from the NHS.

Check any:

  • prescriptions
  • test results
  • hospital referral letters
  • appointment letters

If you cannot find your NHS number

Ask at your GP surgery if you cannot find your NHS number. They should be able to tell you what it is.

If you do not have a GP, you’ll need to register with a GP surgery to get an NHS number.

Find a local GP surgery to register with.
