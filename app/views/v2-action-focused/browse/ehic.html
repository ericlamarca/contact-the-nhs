EHIC (European Health Insurance Card)

Get a European Health Insurance Card (EHIC)

A valid European Health Insurance Card (EHIC) gives you the right to access state-provided healthcare during a temporary stay in another European Economic Area (EEA) country or Switzerland.

Apply for an EHIC

You can also find out more about who can get an EHIC

Replacing a lost or stolen EHIC

If you're in the UK

If you need to replace your EHIC while you're in the UK, call 0300 330 1350

If you're abroad

You'll need to get a Provisional Replacement Certificate instead.

Phone Overseas Healthcare Services on +44 191 218 1999, Monday to Friday, 8am to 5pm. If you need to request a PRC outside these hours, call as soon as you can the next working day.

When calling for a PRC, you'll need to provide your:

  • National Insurance number
  • name
  • address
  • date of birth
  • NHS number, if possible
  • the email address or fax number for the specific department of the hospital or service where you're receiving treatment
